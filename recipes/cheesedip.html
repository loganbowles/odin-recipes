Cheese Dip

Introduction

This cheese dip has all the best ingredients! I compiled it from various other recipes and it's my favorite by far. Serve as a dip at a party or with sour cream and jalape√±os to make nachos.

Ingredients

  • Two 16oz blocks of cheese
  • Ground beef
  • 1 medium onion
  • Cream cheese
  • Two cans of tomatoes
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef until done and remove from the pan
  2. Cook diced onion until soft
  3. Return meat to pan and combine with onion and cream cheese
  4. Then insert milk, tomatoes, and blocks of cheese
  5. Cover the pot and cook until all cheese has melted
