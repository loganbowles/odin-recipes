Sausage Balls

Introduction

This cheese dip has all the best ingredients! I compiled it from various other recipes and it's my favorite by far. Serve as a dip at a party or with sour cream and jalape√±os to make nachos.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of sausage
  • 16oz cheese
  • Pancake mix
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl
  2. Roll the mixture into a tablespoon size ball
  3. Place on a pan and cook for 20 minutes at 350 degrees
