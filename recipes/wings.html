Introduction

This cheese dip has all the best ingredients! I compiled it from various other recipes and it's my favorite by far. Serve as a dip at a party or with sour cream and jalape√±os to make nachos.

Ingredients

  • Pack of chicken wings
  • Your choice of hot wing sauce
  • Baking powder

Steps

  1. Cut the wings if not already seperated
  2. Place into a bowl and pat dry
  3. Sprinkle 1 tablespoon of baking powder onto the wings
  4. Cover with wing sauce for at least 1 hour
  5. Place the wings on the grill for 25 minutes and remove
